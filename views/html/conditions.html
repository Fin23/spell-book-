Conditions

Death Saving Throw: roll a flat d20 no modifier, three rolls above 10 stabilizes, three rolls under 10 you die, roll a 1 counts as 2 fails, roll 20 automatically alive with 1 hp, damage past 0 that equals your full health auto death, party member can administer first aid to stabilize must roll DC 10 or hirer Wisdom / Medicine check + modifier, if stabilized you remain at 0 hp and stay unconscious but no longer need to roll death saving throws
Food : Can survive with out food for 3 days + Con Mod, beyond that you become "exhausted"
Water: 1 - 2 gallons per day depending on heat, after that must make DC 15 Con saving throw, or become increasingly "exhausted" per day with out water
Falling: 1d6 bludgeoning damager per 10ft max 20d6, lands prone
Suffocating: can hold breath for 1min + Con mod past that can survive rounds equal to con mod before dying flat out
