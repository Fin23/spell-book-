Food : Can survive with out food for 3 days + Con Mod, beyond that you become "exhausted"
Water: 1 - 2 gallons per day depending on heat, after that must make DC 15 Con saving throw, or become increasingly "exhausted" per day with out water
Falling: 1d6 bludgeoning damager per 10ft max 20d6, lands prone
Suffocating: can hold breath for 1min + Con mod past that can survive rounds equal to con mod before dying flat out
